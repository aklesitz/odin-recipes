Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A classic favorite! Just like mom used to make!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (strawberry or grape, to personal taste)
  • Knife

Steps

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter over one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly over other slice of bread
  4. Combine bread, ingredient side together
