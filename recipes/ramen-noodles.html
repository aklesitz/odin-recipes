Ramen Noodles

Description

Super high in sodium, these dirt cheap noodles will straight up kill you. Featuring an impressive entire weeks worth of salt crammed into just one sitting, these are barely fit for human consumption but hey, at least they only cost like thirty five cents.

Ingredients

  • Pack of Ramen noodles
  • Water
  • Stove top

Steps

  1. Fill saucepan with water and bring to boil
  2. Open packet of ramen noodles
  3. Place disgusting brick of dried noodles into boiling water
  4. Cook until less disgusting looking
  5. At some point throw in little packet of salt powder that came with the ramen noodles (open package first)
